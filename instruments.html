Transient Astrophysics Probe (TAP)

Instruments

TAP is comprised of 4 instruments that work together to survey the sky and follow-up transient and variable sources in the near infrared, X-ray, and gamma-ray bands.

  • Wide Field Imager (WFI) - soft X-ray wide field of view Lobster microchannel optic surveys the sky
  • X-ray Telescope (XRT) - sensitive crystal silicon grazing incidence mirror soft X-ray telescope follows up and localizes transients as they fade
  • InfraRed Telescope (IRT) - near-IR telescope provides localizations and rapid low-resolution spectroscopy of new transients
  • Gamma-ray Transient Monitor (GTM) - NaI scintillators coupled ot PMTs monitoring the sky for transient hard X-ray and gamma-ray sources
